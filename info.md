# Home Activity Intelligence

Not just an event log — operational analytics for your Home Assistant that explains **who**, **when**, and **why** something happens at home.

Tracks every interactive action, classifies it as user / automation / script / system, attributes the right person and the right automation, and surfaces dead automations, conflicts, duplicates, and routine candidates.

Comes with sensors, REST API, a Lovelace card, and a polished sidebar panel. Zero configuration. Local SQLite. No telemetry.
